About Us

Welcome to GroundUp. GroundUp is a trusted community marketplace, providing full range of customer-to-customer or business-to-customer sales services via the Internet. Ground Up connects people to unique services, at any price point. With our highly trained customer service and a growing community of users, Ground Up is the easiest way for people to monetize their skilled services and showcase it to an audience of millions.


Our mission is to connect contractors and clients together in our project marketplace. Clients use GroundUp to post listings for tasks that they need performed. Contractors can then bid on jobs, with GroundUp providing ratings from previous customers to ensure clients get reliable, high-quality services. Whether you're considering a whole house renovation or you are crafting an interactive website, no job is too large or too small to be listed on GroundUp.

Relationship

We unite people that would unlikely meet otherwise, building long-term relationships and trust. GroundUp intelligently suggests potential contractors based on patterns and trends from past completed services.

Transparency

We make it a priority to give clients as much information about their contractor as possible. We allow people who have had work done with the contractor previously to rate their work, which determines their reputation score, and contractors can show their relevant certifications and licences.

Customer Satisfaction

We believe in quality work and to ensure that contractors conform to customer’s standards. We have set up our system so that clients deposit the money required for a job to GroundUp and be paid after the work has been performed.
